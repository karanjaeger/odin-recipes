Fish & Chips Recipe

Description

Fish and chips is a traditional British dish consisting of fried fish and thinly sliced fried potatoes.
Simple fish and chips made with cod fillets and twice-fried fries.
Tastes great as-is or with malt vinegar, lemon, or tartar sauce.
